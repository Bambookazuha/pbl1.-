Volunteer Roles

01

Garden Maintenance

Help with watering, weeding, composting, and keeping the garden tidy and healthy.

02

Workshop Assistant

Support gardening or sustainability workshops by setting up materials and helping participants.

03

Composting Coordinator

Manage compost bins and guide others on how to compost properly.

04

Seed & Plant Exchange

Organize donated seeds and plants, and help run exchange events for the community.

05

Community Outreach

Promote events, recruit volunteers, and build local connections through flyers and social media.

06

Accessibility Support

Assist visitors with mobility or language needs so everyone can join garden activities.
