Plants you grow can become part of your daily meals!

From crisp lettuce and juicy tomatoes to fragrant herbs like basil and mint, growing your own produce means fresher, healthier ingredients right at your fingertips. It's rewarding, sustainable, and delicious.

Tomatoes

Rich in vitamins A and C, tomatoes are easy to grow and perfect for salads and sauces.

Spinach

Leafy green packed with iron and nutrients. Easy to grow in containers or garden beds.

Carrots

Crunchy and sweet, carrots are a root vegetable that can be grown in deep, loose soil.

Lettuce

Quick to grow and great for salads, sandwiches, and wraps.

Natural Plant-Based Dyes

Natural plant-based dyes offer an eco-friendly and sustainable alternative to synthetic colors. Derived from leaves, roots, fruits, and seeds, these dyes bring unique, earthy tones to fabrics while minimizing environmental impact. Turmeric

Produces a vibrant yellow dye that can be used for fabrics and paper. Mix with alum for more lasting effects.

Beetroot

Creates a stunning pink or reddish-purple dye, ideal for eco-friendly craft projects and fabric coloring.

Hibiscus

Yields a soft pink or lavender hue, perfect for adding a subtle, natural tint to textiles.

Indigo

A deep blue dye traditionally used in denim production. Extracted from indigofera plants.

Spinach

Provides a gentle green shade, suitable for small crafts and light fabrics.

Avocado

Creates a soft peachy-pink tone using avocado skins and pits. A sustainable dye source.
