BASIC TOOLS

FOR YOUR GARDEN JOURNEY

Gloves

Soft, protective gloves that keep your hands clean and safe while pulling weeds, planting seeds, or working with soil.

Water Gear

Helpful tools like a watering can or hose that make it easy to give your plants the right amount of water every day.

Garden Fork

A sturdy fork great for loosening soil, turning compost, or breaking up clumps in pots and garden beds.

Shovel

A strong digging tool perfect for moving soil, planting trees, or clearing out space in larger garden areas.

Bucket

A lightweight bucket ideal for carrying soil, compost, water, or garden tools around your outdoor space.

Trowel

A small digging tool perfect for planting seeds, moving small plants, or digging holes for flowers in pots or garden beds.

Shears

Sharp cutting tools used for trimming plants, shaping bushes, or removing dead leaves and branches neatly.

Kneeler

A soft kneeling pad that helps protect your knees and makes gardening more comfortable when working close to the ground.
